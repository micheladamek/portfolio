Case Study № 05 — Fintech

NOWO.

Making pension savings somethingyou actually want to do.

Client — Nowo Role — UX & Product Design
Scope Onboarding · Savings Features · Visual Identity
Platform Nowo App, iOS & Android
Deliverables Shipped UX + Refreshed Identity
The Brief

Pension saving is important, distant and dull. Nowo's promise flips it: put pocket change away today, for financial freedom tomorrow. Our job was to make saving feel like a pleasure — not a chore.

Nowo is a forward-thinking fund company on a mission to make retirement savings a source of financial peace of mind rather than anxiety. Together with my colleagues, I worked with Nowo to refine the product — enhancing the experience for savers and encouraging the kind of long-term usage pension saving actually requires.

The primary objective was simple to state and hard to do: motivate people to save more. The answer wasn't pressure — it was delight. We introduced interactive features that offered insightful, joyful ways of saving, wrapped in a brand that talks like a person.

The Turning Point

User interviews kept surfacing the same motivation: people don't fall in love with saving — they fall in love with watching their money grow.

The insight. What savers valued most was witnessing growth — seeing what their money was becoming, and what it could be worth in the future. The numbers mattered less than the feeling of momentum.

The decision. Growth became the product's emotional core, and every barrier to reaching it was lowered. The clearest example: onboarding went from an extensive form to a friendly chat — a conversation that positions Nowo as supportive and secure from the very first minute.

The Work

Four moves

  • 01

    Chat Onboarding

    Replaced an extensive sign-up form with an intuitive chat interface — supportive, secure, and human from the first tap.

  • 02

    New Ways to Save

    Daily saves, salary saves, a percentage of every purchase — small, playful mechanics that turn saving into a habit.

  • 03

    Voice & Tone

    A novel communication approach — personal, relatable and genuinely funny, in a category that usually sounds like fine print.

  • 04

    Visual Identity

    A revamped digital identity — new typeface, updated illustrations and color palette — for a more modern, more fun experience.

The Results

Saving, reinvented

  • A significant shift in user saving patterns after relaunch
  • An influx of savers committing to their long-term financial future
  • More people saving more — and in entirely new ways
Looking Back

Three things
I keep

01

Delight moves money

Joy isn't decoration in fintech — it changed behavior. People saved more because the product made saving feel rewarding right now, not in forty years.

02

Talk like a person

The chat onboarding and a tone with actual humor outperformed the standard form-and-fine-print approach. In a low-trust category, sounding human is a feature.

03

Fun needs proof to lean on

Playfulness only works when security is visible next to it. Every joke in the interface earns its place by sitting on top of credibility — never instead of it.
