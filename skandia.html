Case Study № 06 — Banking

SKANDIA.

Two years rebuilding how a bankfeels in your hand.

Client — Skandia Role — UX & Visual Design, 2+ years
Scope Mobile App · My Account Portal · Design System
Domain Banking, Insurance, Pension & Funds
Constraints MiFID · WCAG 2.0 AA · GDPR
The Brief

Skandia left its physical visiting centers behind and went digital-first. Which means the app and the logged-in portal stopped being channels — they became the bank.

Skandia spans loans, retirement planning, insurance, funds, stocks and banking. Going digital-first raised the stakes for every screen: the mobile app needed a new design language, and "My Account" — the logged-in heart of the bank — needed a complete overhaul from table-heavy, non-responsive layouts into a modern, responsive experience.

The brief had a built-in tension, captured in one line from the project: make the app feel as Skandia as possible while maintaining a native app experience. Brand warmth and platform conventions, both at once — under banking regulation (MiFID, GDPR) and accessibility requirements the whole way.

The Work

Three fronts

  • 01

    Mobile App

    A redesign weaving Skandia's brand into native iOS and Android patterns — familiar as a bank, comfortable as an app.

  • 02

    My Account

    The logged-in portal rebuilt from dense tables into a responsive experience, with conversion flows optimized for private and business customers.

  • 03

    Design System

    A comprehensive Sketch symbols library keeping app, portal and site speaking one visual language.

The Results

One bank, one language

  • A redesigned app blending Skandia identity with native platform conventions
  • My Account transformed from table-heavy pages into a modern, responsive portal
  • A design system that kept two years of work — and every team after — consistent
  • All of it shipped within banking regulation and accessibility requirements
