Case Study № 09 — Public Sector

SOCIAL- STYRELSEN.

A design system for the authoritybehind Sweden's health and care.

Client — Socialstyrelsen Role — UX & Design Systems
Client The National Board of Health and Welfare
Deliverable Design System — Figma · Storybook
Audience Designers · Developers · Editors · PMs
The Brief

The authority overseeing Sweden's health care and social services builds digital things across many departments. One design system had to serve them all.

Socialstyrelsen — Sweden's National Board of Health and Welfare — needed to streamline how designers, developers, web editors and project managers build together. The work began by understanding the people the system would serve: interviews with key staff, plus conversations with other organizations that had walked the same path.

From that research came a clear proposal for scope and solution — then the system itself: a style guide covering colors, typography and grid; UI components from buttons to forms to navigation; templates, accessibility guidelines and documentation. Components were coded and published in Storybook, so the system lives where developers work, not in a PDF.

The Results

Built to be used

  • A fully functional design system delivered across Figma and Storybook
  • Consistent design and development workflows across departments
  • Accessibility guidance embedded in the system — the public-sector floor, by design
