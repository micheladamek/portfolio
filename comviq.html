Case Study № 08 — Telecom

COMVIQ.

The framework behind every pixelof comviq.se.

Client — Comviq Role — Digital Experience Manager
Scope Digital Corporate Identity — comviq.se
Role Digital Experience Manager, all brand channels
Audience Designers · Developers · PMs · Analysts
The Brief

Dozens of people build comviq.se — designers, developers, marketers, analysts. To enable all of them, there had to be one solid framework to build from.

As Digital Experience Manager I was responsible for the digital customer experience across all of Comviq's brand channels and platforms. The cornerstone was a comprehensive digital corporate identity: digital elements, pixel-perfected distances, and the numerous templates that let everyone touching comviq.se build the same site.

The job was as much orchestration as design — working across UX designers, SEO specialists, analysts, graphic designers, frontend and backend developers, marketing and product owners to assure quality and keep the experience cohesive through every channel and format.

The Work

The framework

  • 01

    Identity System

    Digital elements and pixel-perfected specifications — the single source of truth for how Comviq looks and behaves online.

  • 02

    Templates

    Reusable, standardized templates serving designers, developers, project managers and new employees alike.

  • 03

    Quality Orchestration

    Cross-functional collaboration keeping every channel and format coherent — from campaign pages to checkout.

The Results

One brand, every channel

  • A digital corporate identity adopted across everyone building comviq.se
  • Standardized templates that onboard new team members into the brand fast
  • A cohesive customer experience across all channels and formats
